About Me

In case you haven't realized already, my name is Daniel Parker! I am currently an undergradute student at West Chester University going for my bachelor's degree in Computer Science with an Astronomy minor on the side. I am on track to graduating from West Chester University in spring 2022. Over the years, I have been fine-tuning my programming skills by making small games in Unity with C#. I have yet to fully publish a video game, but I have a few in the works that you can check out here.

Little List of Skills

  • Java
  • C#
  • Intermediate understanding of the Unity game engine
  • HTML and CSS
